Priyanka Chopra, Nick Jonas, and Malti Enjoy Family Getaway in Dubai

Publish date: 2024-08-10

Not even three years old yet, Priyanka Chopra and Nick Jonas’s daughter, Malti Marie, is already a world traveler. The toddler recently accompanied her parents on a family getaway to Dubai, in the United Arab Emirates, which Chopra documented on her Instagram, including some rare photos of Malti in one post.

“Home away from home,” the Citadel star wrote in her caption. “Thank you @bulgarihotels for being such excellent hosts last weekend ♥️ #throwback #ad.”

In the first snap, Chopra and Jonas are seen snuggling up on a boat, and despite not being pictured, Malti is assumedly not far away, given the pacifier stuck on Jonas’s finger. Besides the stylish binky, Jonas also wears a cream button-up with a print of a tennis player on the front. The collar features orange and green stripes—the latter of which Jonas matches with his bright lime shades from Burberry—and underneath the top, he sports a white ribbed tank. Meanwhile, Chopra wears an animal-print slipdress, which she layers under an eggshell-colored wrap.

Throughout the rest of the carousel, we see glimpses of the family vacay, including rare peeks at Malti. One shot sees Chopra playfully throwing the toddler into the air, while a following video sees Malti playing in the sand in a black-and-white striped dress covered in a lemon print. For good measure, Chopra throws in a snap of Jonas standing by the shore in a T-shirt that reads “Miracle Academy,” which he pairs with sweatpants and a New York Yankees baseball cap.

The Quantico alum closed out the post with a snap of her view from a lounge chair: a calm, sunny beach.

The Hollywood pair got together in 2018, and married that same year, in December. They welcomed Malti via surrogate in January 2022.
