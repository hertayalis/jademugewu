About

NFL inside linebacker that was first signed by the Houston Texans as an undrafted free agent in 2017. He was later signed by the Tennessee Titans in March 2022. He began playing with the Chicago Bears in 2023.

He previously played college football at Missouri State University. Following his 2016 season, he was named to the first-team All-MVFC.

He made his official NFL debut playing against the Jacksonville Jaguars during week one of the 2017 regular season. He recorded 29 tackles in 12 games played during his rookie season.

Although he was born in Springfield, Missouri, he grew up living with his family in Rogersville, Missouri. He is one of four siblings.

He played alongside Joshua Dobbs as a member of the Titans during the 2022 NFL season.
