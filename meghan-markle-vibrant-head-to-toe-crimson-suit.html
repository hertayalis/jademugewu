See Meghan Markle in a Vibrant, Head-to-Toe Crimson Suit

Publish date: 2024-06-15

Duchess Meghan began her week of philanthropic events with a bold monochromatic look.

The Duke and Duchess of Sussex attended the One Young World Summit in Manchester, England, today, where they met with young leaders from around the globe. For the event's opening ceremony, Meghan wore a bright-red professional look by the sustainable brand Another Tomorrow, comprised of a long-sleeved silk blouse, which featured a matching torso-length tie knotted at the neck, and with matching straight-leg trousers.

The duchess finished the look with matching pointed-toe Aquazzura heels. She also wore her hair in a slick, low ponytail, and accessorized the outfit with chunky gold earrings and a matching ring by Tabayer.

Meanwhile, Prince Harry wore a traditional navy suit with a white shirt, dress shoes, and a matching navy tie.

OLI SCARFF//Getty Images

Chris Jackson//Getty Images

Before the ceremony, Meghan was spotted heading to the event in a chic business casual look. She wore loose-fitting tan trousers with a taupe silk cashmere sweater by Brandon Maxwell, featuring short sleeves and oversized buttons.

Last spring, the duchess wore a stunning monochromatic look for the opening of the Invictus Games last April. She looked radiant in an all-white suit and a matching handbag by Valentino, which both featured gold hardware, finishing the look with same Aquazzura heels she wore to her wedding to Harry.

Max Mumby/Indigo//Getty Images

Harry and Meghan arrived in the U.K. on Saturday morning accompanied by a team of Archewell aides and private security, with Hello! Magazine reporting that the team flew commercial. They made the trip without their children Archie, 3, and Lili, 1.

A spokesperson for The Duke and Duchess of Sussex previously told BAZAAR.com that the couple would "visit with several charities close to their hearts in early September," with stops in Manchester, Düsseldorf, and London.
