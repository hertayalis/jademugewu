Sadie Calvano

Sadie Calvano is an American actress. She had her career beginnings in the 2010s. Initially, she made one-time roles in NCIS, Eagleheart, Kickin’ It, and Crash & Bernstein. In 2011, she made her big screen debut with a minor role in the biographical drama J. Edgar. She later appeared as Keira in 3 episodes of the sitcom Melissa & Joey. Calvano’s breakthrough role was as Violet Plunkett in the CBS sitcom Mom, a role she played from 2013 to 2016. She returned to the show in 2018 as a guest star. She next starred in the TV movie The Perfect Daughter. The actress went on to star in the straight-to-video movie The Package. She recently starred as April in the web series Why Women Kill.
