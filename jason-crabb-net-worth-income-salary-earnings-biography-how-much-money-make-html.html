Jason Crabb (born March 3, 1977) is a Christian music singer and musician who specialises in contemporary Christian music. He is the main singer for the rock band The Crabb Family, which he founded. Crabb was named “Favorite Male Vocalist” at the inaugural annual Harmony Honors Awards, as well as “Favorite Young Artist” at the 2000 Singing News Fan Awards. Crabb has also been nominated for a Grammy Award. In 2004, he was named Male Vocalist of the Year in Gospel Music for the year 200Throughout his career, he has been nominated for a slew of additional accolades. As a soloist, he has performed with the Brooklyn Tabernacle Choir on a number of occasions.
