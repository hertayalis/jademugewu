Republican politician who was elected the junior US Senator from West Virginia in 2015. She previously served as the US Representative of West Virginia's 2nd district between 2001 and 2015.

She earned a degree in zoology from Duke University. She later graduated with a master's degree in education from the University of Virginia.

She served in the West Virginia House of Delegates between 1996 and 2000. She was a finalist for the vice presidency for then-presidential candidate Donald Trump in 2016.

She has three kids with her husband, Charles.
