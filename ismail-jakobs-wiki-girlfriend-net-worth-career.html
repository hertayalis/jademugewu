How did Ismail Jakobs start his Professional Career?

At BC Bliesheim, Jakobs started his career. He began participating in FC Koln’s youth program in 2012. He participated in all of the club’s minor teams until being selected for the U21 squad in 2017, which competed in the Regionalliga West, a fourth-tier league.

Jakobs was promoted to the first team prior to the 2019–20 season by the club’s manager at the time, Achim Beierlorzer. On November 8, 2019, he took the field against TSG Hoffenheim for the first time.

Jakobs scored his first goal as a professional on December 18, 2019, in a 2-4 loss for Eintracht Frankfurt. In March 2020, the team informed Jakobs that his contract had been extended through 2022. He signed a second deal in October of that same year, keeping him in Koln through July of 2024.

Jakobs agreed to a deal with AS Monaco in Ligue 1 on July 12, 2021, that would extend until 2026. He made his team debut on August 3 when they defeated Sparta Prague 2-0 in the UEFA Champions League third qualifying round.
